Artsfest

The following is poster design I created for the University of Wisconsin La Crosse Art Department's ArtsFest.

  • Date: November 2022
  • Client: UWL Art Department
  • Role: Poster Designer
  • www.uwlax.edu/creative-imperatives/

Client Needs

  • Poster Competition

    Design a poster for the newly renamed "ArtsFest"

  • Theme

    Conform to the theme "A Beautiful Remedy for Today"

  • Audience

    Appeal to students of University of Wisconsin La Crosse

Design Decisions

  • Typography

    A Beautiful Remedy for Today: A bold yet friendly handmade font.

    Dosis: A curvy sans-serif font used for the remainder of the poster.

  • Why?

    A Beautiful Remedy for Today: Bold to grab the attention of students passing by bulletin boards, yet friendly to match the feel of the event.

    Dosis: Contrasts the boldness of the headline. Curvy to match the feel of ArtsFest.

Design Decisions

  • Main Graphic

    “I felt the best way to emphasize the role of art as a remedy was to visually express the two as literally being hand in hand – firmly supporting each other”

Design Decisions

  • Colors

    A red, teal, and cream combination reminiscent of vintage print posters.

  • Why?

    Plays on the current vintage-aesthetic trend amongst college students.
