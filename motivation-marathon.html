Motivation Marathon

This case study follows a poster I made for a hypothetical sports and motivation company named 'Elevate Elite'. To practice my design skills, I had Chat GPT be my 'client', and I used its pitch to create a poster for an event called 'Motivation Marathon'.

  • Date: August 2023
  • Client: Chat GPT
  • Role: Poster Designer
  • www.chatgpt.com

Client Pitch

  • Audience

    "Athletes of all fitness levels, fitness enthusiasts, and individuals seeking motivation to improve their physical and mental well-being."

  • Needed Info

    Tagline: Rise Beyond Boundaries
    Quote: "The only limit is you"
    Event info: Date and location

  • Focal Image

    "A dynamic and impactful visual representation of an athlete pushing themselves to excel. This image should capture the essence of determination, passion, and the relentless pursuit of excellence."

Event Headline

  • Motivation Marathon

    I hand-crafted a bold font for the event headline because Bold fonts are heavily used in modern design especially within sporting companies (Nike, Puma, and Dicks Sporting Goods are a few who come to mind that thrive on thick sans-serif fonts). On top of this, the blockiness of this font is meant to reinforce the idea that athletes are solid, resilient individuals. The negative space that snakes between the letters also resembles the routes and paths that runners spend much of their time on.

Color Choices

  • Analagous Color Scheme

    Orange stands as the most prominent color because orange is often associated with movement in graphic design theory. From here, I decided to keep the color scheme warm to maintain a feeling of action and motivation for the event. The yellow worked nice as a contrasting light color, which helped me bring focus to the most important areas of the poster: the event headline and the main image.

Layout and Imagery

  • Rule of Thirds

    The areas where I want the viewer to look first fall upon the 'thirds' of the poster.

  • Contrast

    The areas where I want the viewer to look first also have the most contrast. They stand in an accent yellow. Furthermore, the watch utilizes contrast of shape depicting a circle rather than a square or straight line such as in the rest of the design.
